About us

An honest garage run by the same family for thirty years

Morgan Richards Garage was founded in 1994 in Woolwich and has been based at Anchor & Hope Lane in Charlton for the past fifteen years. In 2024, we marked thirty years in business — three decades of the same team, the same standards, and the same commitment to SE London drivers. Richard and his team have built the business on a straightforward principle: do the work properly, explain it clearly, and only charge for what the car actually needs.

We are AutoCare registered and carry RMI membership, meaning every job comes with manufacturer-backed part guarantees and an independent standard of service. We have invested in IMI certification and Nexdrive approval for electric and hybrid vehicles, because that is where SE London's fleet is heading and we intend to be ready for it.

We respond to every Google review ourselves. If something went wrong, Richard wants to know about it. If something went well, he wants to say thank you. That is not a marketing strategy — it is just how a local business should operate.

What to expect

We call you before starting any work beyond what was agreed. You see the old worn parts before we charge for replacements — no trust required, just transparency. All calls with the garage are recorded for quality and compliance. You pay when the work is done.
